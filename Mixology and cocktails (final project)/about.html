WHAT IS MIXOLOGY?

Blending your spirit with ours!

Is Mixology just a fancy way to speak about Bartending, or is it something different? We are about to find out.

Mixology can be defined as the art and study of preparing, inventing and serving mixed drinks, the so-called cocktails; the in-depth stud y of the craft of mixing drinks.

Molecular mixology is the process of creating cocktails using the equipment and techniques of molecular gastronomy. The equipment that is used varies from the simplest items such as blowtorches, shaker, bar spoon to items such as vacuum sealer and atomizer.
